How to apply

Dressing a wireframe

1 · Cream canvas, white cards

Every page is #F5EFE8. Content groups become #FEFCF8 panels at radius 28px with the soft md shadow.

2 · Indigo by default, burgundy for intent

Headings & primary actions are indigo. Reserve burgundy for the single most-wanted action and for the active/selected state.

3 · Heavy display, quiet body

Lead with 800-weight grotesque set tight. Keep body and meta at 400 and muted so the display does the talking.

4 · Round everything, shadow softly

Pills for chips/toggles, 14px for buttons/fields, 20–28px for cards. Borders are warm hairlines, never grey.
